Smart Lid

Drop-in replacement for the Weck 743 wooden lid — just with a brain inside

★ Recommended Design Weck 743 · 25.3 fl oz
Why This Design Wins

It's the same gesture you already do — lift lid, feed, replace lid. Zero learning curve. The lid looks and feels like the wooden one you're replacing, just slightly thicker. Glance at the e-ink display anytime without pulling out your phone.

Form

Two-Shell Enclosure

The lid is a two-piece PETG sandwich — a top shell (with e-ink window and LED) and a bottom shell (with ToF sensor mount and gasket groove). They screw or snap together with the PCB and battery sandwiched inside. The top can be printed in wood-tone PETG filament to mimic the original Weck wooden lid aesthetic, or you could glue a real wood veneer disc on top.

Display

E-Ink at a Glance

A 1.54" e-ink screen sits flush in the top surface. It shows rise percentage, current temperature, and estimated time to peak. Because e-ink holds its image with zero power draw, it only uses energy when the display updates (every 15 min). Perfect for a battery-powered device that mostly sleeps.

Power

Rechargeable LiPo

A 500mAh flat-pack battery gives 7-10 days of life at 15-minute measurement intervals. The ESP32-C3 deep-sleeps between readings, waking only to measure, update the e-ink, and send data over WiFi. Charge via USB-C — just pull the lid off the jar and plug it in overnight.
